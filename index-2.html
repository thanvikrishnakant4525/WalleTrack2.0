Frequently Ask Question

We’re here to help you! Whether it’s about features, pricing, or how WalleTrack works — feel free to reach out.

What is WalleTrack?

WalleTrack is a smart money management app designed for students and young professionals to track expenses, save money, and build better financial habits.

Do I need to pay to use WalleTrack?

No, WalleTrack offers multiple plans — you can start with the Basic Plan and upgrade to Standard or Premium for advanced features.

How does WalleTrack help me save money?

It categorizes your expenses, sets budgets, and gives insights into your spending patterns so you can cut unnecessary costs.

Is my data safe with WalleTrack?

Yes! We use secure encryption and privacy-first policies to ensure your personal and financial data stays protected.

Can I use WalleTrack on multiple devices?

Absolutely. You can log in on your phone, tablet, or desktop and sync your data seamlessly.
